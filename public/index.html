/API/CHESS/GETBESTMOVE

Example Request: curl -X POST 'https://minhhoang.info/api/chess/getbestmove' -d 'fen=3r1rk1/5ppp/2p1pn2/p1P5/1p2P3/3Q1P2/PP3P1P/3RR1K1 b&time=100'

fen: FEN of board (ex: 3r1rk1/5ppp/2p1pn2/p1P5/1p2P3/3Q1P2/PP3P1P/3RR1K1 b)

time: Go move time ( The time limit for a chess engine to calculate a move is set. 1000 equals 1 second, and the maximum limit is 10 seconds, which is equivalent to 10000. ex: 100)

Response: {"bestMove":"d8d3"}
